Our Donation Plan

Our organization has an elaborate donation plan to help the needy in all spheres of society.Our goal is to ensure
Basic Human Rights for everyone

Food for family

Food is scarce in such a nation as ours.Providing healthy and nutritious foods and suplements to the children and the pregnant women are our primary objective.We focus on the preganant women and children from malnourishment and diseases.

Shelter for a family

Providing shelter to the homeless people is one of the many projects that have been carried out by our organization,We are currently running 5 shelter home for the homeless people and we also relocate the families in the disaster stricken area

Education for a child

Providing quality education to deprived children is our topmost priority. Without education the children of the nation will be burden to us.There are currently 10 voluntary schools run by our organization where education is free for all
